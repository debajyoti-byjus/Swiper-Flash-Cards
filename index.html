Laws of Reflection

Key Points

  • Incident ray, Reflected Ray and Normal: all lie on the same plane
  • Angle of Incidnece = Angle of Reflection

Types of Reflection

Key points

  • There are two types of reflection: Regular and Irregular reflection.
  • Both Regular and Irregular reflection follow the laws of reflection.

Reflection by a plane Mirror

Key points

    Nature of the image :
  • Same distance
  • Same size
  • Virtual
  • Erect
  • Laterally Inverted

Minimum length of mirror needed to see full image of a person

Key points

  • The minimum length of a plane mirror needed, to enable a person to see his full image, is half half his height

Minimum length of mirror to see full image of the wall

Key points

  • Minimum length of the mirror required by a person, standing midway between a wall and a mirror, to see the full height of the wall behind him is, h/3, where h is the height of the wall
