Alpha Particles

Are the Alpha particles emitted from a radioactive material just Helium Nuclei?

Answer

Yes! Alpha particles, also called alpha rays or alpha radiation, consist of two protons and two neutrons bound together into a particle identical to a helium-4 nucleus.
